About Us

The Shardaabai Khemchand Chomal Charitable Trust was established to honor the memory of Late Smt. Shardaabai Khemchandji Chomal, a dedicated philanthropist whose lifelong commitment to the welfare of children, healthcare for the elderly, and the advancement of religious activities across India continues to inspire our endeavors.

In keeping with her noble legacy, the trust is devoted to providing quality education to children from all sectors of society, ensuring equitable access to learning opportunities. We are equally committed to promoting comprehensive healthcare for individuals of all ages, from young children to the elderly, while fostering healthier lifestyle practices. Through structured programs, we aim to enhance community well-being, support sustainable development, and uphold spiritual values. Guided by principles of transparency, compassion, and excellence, the trust strives to perpetuate Smt. Shardaabai’s vision of transforming lives and strengthening communities throughout India.

Our Mission

Education

We empower students in grades 4–12 by bridging gaps between formal schooling and self-directed learning. Our programs foster critical thinking through engaging activities and games, while enhancing proficiency in mathematics and English communication. We provide specialized training for dedicated students to excel in Olympiad-level mathematics, guide them in creating innovative math projects, and conduct online and offline seminars in selected regions. These initiatives support children who aspire to grow but face challenges accessing such opportunities. Additionally, we promote holistic child development through diverse educational activities, nurturing creativity and confidence.

Healthcare

We are dedicated to promoting holistic healthcare for individuals of all ages, from children to the elderly, by providing access to essential medical services and preventive care. Our programs emphasize natural healing by imparting knowledge on healthy lifestyles, balanced nutrition, and optimal sleep practices. Through community workshops and outreach, we empower individuals to adopt sustainable habits that enhance physical and mental well-being. By collaborating with local organizations, we deliver impactful health initiatives that uphold compassion and support long-term health outcomes across India.
