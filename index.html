About Us

The Shardaabai Khemchand Chomal Charitable Trust was established in memory of Late Smt. Shardaabai Khemchandji Chomal, a compassionate visionary who dedicated her life to the welfare of children, healthcare for the elderly, and the promotion of religious activities across India.

Inspired by her legacy, we work tirelessly to uplift communities all over India through impactful programs rooted in transparency, integrity, and service. Our initiatives continue to honor her contributions by fostering positive change and empowering those in need.

Our Mission

Empowerment

We strive to empower individuals and communities through education, skill development, and access to essential resources.

Sustainability

Our programs foster sustainable development, ensuring long-term benefits for the communities we serve.
